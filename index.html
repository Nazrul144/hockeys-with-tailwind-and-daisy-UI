Junior Program

This is the Junior program section of Hockey sport. The term "junior hockey" typically
refers to a level of ice hockey played by young athletes who are usually in their late

Professional Program

Professional hockey generally refers to the highest level of play within the sport, where athletes
are compensated for their participation. The game is divided into three periods of 20 minutes
playing time each, with a 15-minute intermission between periods.
